Maharashtra

“Arrey, Maharashtra is like a full combo state, yaar. You get everything here – mega city vibe, ancient caves, beaches, forts, hill stations, crazy food, Bollywood feel, all in one place

Mumbai alone is an experience – the rush at Marine Drive, night lights near the sea, cutting chai at the tapri, local trains, and that feeling that the whole country’s energy is packed into one city. You see the Gateway of India on one side, skyscrapers on the other, and you realise why people call it the ‘city of dreams’
