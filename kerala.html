Kerala

Hey bro, Kerala? Man, if Kashmir's your snowy thrill, Kerala's the chill vibe you need—God's Own Country with backwaters, beaches, and hills that make you wanna ditch the laptop forever. Picture cruising Alleppey's houseboats at sunset, palm trees swaying, kingfishers diving—pure reset for that coding grind

Backwaters in Alleppey or Kumarakom: lazy houseboat drifts past villages, fish nets, coconuts dropping—feels like time stops. Munnar's tea estates roll endless green, misty mornings perfect for jeep safaris spotting elephants. Varkala or Kovalam's cliffs and golden sands for beach hangs, way chiller than Goa
