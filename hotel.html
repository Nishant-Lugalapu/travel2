Paying Guests

Paying guest places are basically homes where you can stay by paying monthly rent.They give you a room, food, Wi-Fi, and other basic facilities, so it feels comfortable and convenient.It’s different from hotels because a PG feels more homely, and you meet other people living there too.It’s a good option if you want a safe, budget-friendly place to stay for a longer time
